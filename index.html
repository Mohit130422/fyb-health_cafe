Welcome to FYB Health Cafe, A Venture supported by Utsav Seva Foundation a charitable trust with a mission to spread yoga along with mindful eating and healthy lifestyle.

  • Experience food where nutrient-rich ingredients take center stage, from our revitalizing Soups & Salads to healthy chats, glutten free whole wheat sandwiches, pasta and noodles, we offer millet based pizza and healthy smoothies, fresh juices, sugar free desserts.
  • At FYB Health Cafe, we believe that healthy eating should never compromise on taste. Join us in our mission to promote wellness and vitality through nourishing food and positive energy. Whether you're a dedicated yogi, a health-conscious foodie, or simply seeking a delicious meal made with love.
